Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all browser extensions published by Aevorn LLC ("we", "us", "our").

Data Collection

Our extensions are designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All extension data (such as notes, settings, or preferences) is stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local).

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Browsing history or activity
  • Authentication credentials
  • Financial information
  • Location data

Permissions

Our extensions request only the minimum permissions necessary to function. Common permissions include:

  • activeTab — Access the current tab only when you click the extension icon
  • storage — Save your settings and data locally on your device
  • Host permissions — Access specific websites (listed in each extension's manifest) to provide the extension's core functionality

Third-Party Services

Our extensions do not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. No data is shared with or sold to third parties.

License Validation

Some extensions offer premium features activated by a license key. License validation is performed entirely offline on your device using a cryptographic hash. No license data is transmitted to any server.

Data Retention

All data is stored locally and persists until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data. We have no access to your locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Our extensions are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
